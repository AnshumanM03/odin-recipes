Brownie Recipe

Description:

A brownie is a popular baked dessert characterized by its dense, fudgy texture and rich chocolate flavor. They may also include additions like nuts, chocolate chips, or frosting. Originating in the United States, brownies can range from chewy to cake-like, depending on the recipe, and are often enjoyed as a sweet treat or dessert.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Melt Butter
  2. Add chocolate chips to butter and let them melt together
  3. Add sugar to the butter and chocolate mixture
  4. Add vanilla extract to mixture
  5. One by one add eggs to the mixture
  6. Add flour to the mixture and hand mix
  7. Pour mixture into a pan and put into oven for 40 minutes
