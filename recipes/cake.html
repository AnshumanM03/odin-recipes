Cake Recipe

Description:

Chocolate cake is a rich and decadent dessert. It's typically moist and tender, often enhanced with ingredients like butter or oil, and can be layered with fillings such as chocolate ganache, buttercream, or whipped cream. This versatile treat can be topped with various frostings, sprinkles, fruits, or nuts, making it a favorite for celebrations and indulgent moments alike.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Melt Butter
  2. Add cocoa powder to butter and mix them together
  3. Add sugar to the butter and cocoa mixture
  4. Add vanilla extract to mixture
  5. One by one add eggs to the mixture
  6. Add flour to the mixture and hand mix
  7. Pour mixture into a pan and put into oven for 40 minutes
