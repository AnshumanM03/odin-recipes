Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description:

A chocolate chip cookie is a popular type of cookie featuring a dough base mixed with chocolate chips. These cookies are typically sweet and buttery, with a crisp edge and a chewy center. The chocolate chips, made from semi-sweet or milk chocolate, melt slightly during baking, creating pockets of rich chocolate throughout the cookie. Perfect as a snack or dessert, chocolate chip cookies are a timeless favorite enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Melt Butter
  2. Add sugar to the butter and mix them together
  3. Add vanilla extract to mixture
  4. One by one add eggs to the mixture
  5. Add flour to the mixture and hand mix
  6. Add chocolate chips to the dough and hand mix
  7. Create small balls of dough and put them on a pan to bake for 15 minutes
